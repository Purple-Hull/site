PRIVACY PROTOCOL

I. SCOPE

This protocol governs how Purple Hull R/D Entity ("the Organization") collects, processes, and safeguards information from individuals who access this node. The Organization maintains strict data handling procedures consistent with its operational security standards.

II. INFORMATION COLLECTED

The Organization may collect the following categories of information:

  • Transmission data: designation, contact channel, and message content submitted through the secure form
  • Access logs: timestamps, IP addresses, browser identifiers, and navigation patterns
  • Device signatures: operating system, screen resolution, and connection type

No information is collected beyond what is necessary for operational and security purposes.

III. USE OF INFORMATION

Collected information is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to transmissions and access requests
  • Maintaining site security and integrity
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts
  • Internal operational analytics

The Organization does not sell, lease, or distribute personal information to third parties. Information may be disclosed only when required by applicable law.

IV. DATA RETENTION

Transmission data is retained for the duration necessary to fulfill the stated purpose. Access logs are retained for a period not exceeding twelve months. Upon expiration, data is securely purged in accordance with the Organization's information destruction protocols.

V. COOKIES & TRACKING

This site uses minimal, essential cookies required for basic functionality. No third-party advertising or behavioral tracking technologies are employed. The Organization does not participate in cross-site tracking networks.

VI. SECURITY MEASURES

The Organization employs encryption, access controls, and monitoring systems to protect collected information. While no system is impervious, reasonable and appropriate measures are maintained to safeguard data integrity.

VII. YOUR RIGHTS

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information held by the Organization. To exercise these rights, initiate a transmission through the designated secure channel with the subject line "DATA REQUEST."

VIII. MODIFICATIONS

This protocol may be updated without prior notice. Material changes will be reflected in the revision date above. Continued access constitutes acceptance of the current protocol.

For privacy-related inquiries, initiate a transmission through the designated secure channel.
